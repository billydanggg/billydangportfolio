About Me

First and foremost, welcome to my page! Originally from Orange County, California, I currently attend the University of California, Santa Barbara to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Statistics & Data Science. Through rigorous coursework, projects, and self-teaching, I'm hoping to grow both my technical and personal skills as an aspiring Data Scientist & Analyst.

Why Data Science & Analytics

As I continue my education & learning in the field, I am reminded time and time again that data is everywhere. Nearly every part of our lives and the world around us is measured by some metric - and that's where a Data Scientist comes in! Making sense of and leveraging numbers that would otherwise be ambigious are tasks that greatly interest me. Whether that be financial, environmental, or healthcare data, the multidisplinary nature of the field allows me to explore so many different aspects of the real-world all while using interesting new tools like ML algorithms to forecast or predict future trends.
